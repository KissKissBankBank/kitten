# Align

Text alignment utilities.

## Source

[`assets/stylesheets/kitten/utilities/text/_align.scss`](https://github.com/KissKissBankBank/kitten/blob/master/assets/stylesheets/kitten/utilities/text/_align.scss)

## Usage

Utilities to align items according to media-queries (aligns : center, left, right).

`k-u-align-center`
<Canvas>
<p className="k-u-align-center">
  Hello text align center !
</p>
</Canvas>

`k-u-align-left`
<Canvas>
<p className="k-u-align-left">
  Hello text! Still same style ? No, I'm left aligned.
</p>
</Canvas>

`k-u-align-right`
<Canvas>
<p className="k-u-align-right">
  Hello text! I'm on the right.
</p>
</Canvas>
